Seller Dashboard

  • Post bundles to create a new bundle of food for consumers
  • Edit profile to change user credentials
  • Manage bundles to view currently active bundles and check whether they have expired
  • View analytics page to check your statistics on sell through, waste proxy, pricing effectiveness and insights
  • Verify claim codes on your manage reservations page
  • Check predictions for future bundle reservations on your forecasting page to plan ahead
  • Respond to issue reports made by customers about your products.
